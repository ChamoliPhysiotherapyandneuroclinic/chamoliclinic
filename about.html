About Us

We are dedicated to bringing expert physiotherapy services directly to your doorstep or clinic, making care more convenient, personal, and effective.

We Are The Best For

Physiotherapy

Chamoli's Physiotherapy & Neuro Clinic is a trusted name in holistic rehabilitation and wellness care, committed to enhancing the quality of life through expert physiotherapy and neurological treatment. Located in a patient-friendly environment, our clinic offers personalized therapy sessions tailored to your unique health needs.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between pain and recovery, our clinic is led by experienced professionals who specialize in musculoskeletal, neurological, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a neurological condition, or seeking relief from chronic pain, our evidence-based approach ensures effective and lasting results.

At Chamoli’s, we believe in “Healing with Care and Compassion.” Our state-of-the-art equipment, combined with hands-on techniques and progressive therapy plans, help patients regain mobility, strength, and confidence.

We work with you through your healing from start to finish providing a robust program involving combination of multiple therapies / modalities. Also, after treatment is over, we provide support / guidance to prevent injury from repeating / pain from returning. The happiness on our patient's faces is what keeps us going.
